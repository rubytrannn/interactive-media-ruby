Through Post-Atomic Eyes brings together an interdisciplinary group of artists
and scholars to explore the complex legacy of the atomic age in
contemporary art and culture.

In what ways do photography and other lens-based art practices shed light on this
legacy in the 21st century, and how has atomic culture shaped contemporary
intersections of photography, nuclear industries, and military
techno-cultures?

Join us as we explore some of the most urgent issues of our time, from
climate change and the Anthropocene to surveillance culture and the advent
of drone warfare, through a post-atomic lens.
